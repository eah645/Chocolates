Dark Chocolate

Definition: slightly bitter chocolate, of a deep brown color, without added milk.
  • Ingredients: Cacao beans, Sugar, emulsifier (Soy Lecithin) to preserve texture, flavorings (ex: Vanilla)
  • Used as a source of fiber
  • Rich in disease-fighting antioxidants
  • Has more cacao and less sugar than other chocolates - makes it healthier than milk and white chocolate
